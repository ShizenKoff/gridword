How to Play GridWord

  1. Each puzzle hides 5 interlocking words related to the category.
  2. Some letters are pre-filled â€“ use them as anchors.
  3. Click a cell and type to fill letters. Backspace to correct.
  4. Click any top row cell (except the far left) to type down instead of across.
  5. Use Hint to reveal a letter (+10s time penalty).
  6. In Daily mode, everyone gets the same puzzle for the day. Solve it to grow your streak.

Tip: focus on one word at a time. When a word clicks into place, the others often fall like dominoes.
